Who We Are

Vacant House Guy is a Veteran-owned, family-run business that buys unwanted houses in Central New York, Knoxville, TN, Charlotte, NC, and Colorado Springs, CO. Whether you’ve inherited a property, have a house that got away from you, or are just done dealing with a vacant place — we want to hear from you.

We're not a big faceless company. We're real people who understand that sometimes life changes, and a house doesn’t fit anymore. We buy properties in all kinds of shape — from complete tear-downs to turnkey homes. If you’re holding onto a headache, let us make you an offer.

How It Works

  1. You fill out a short form with the basics about the property (address, condition, your contact info).
  2. We take a look — either personally or through someone we trust — usually within 48 hours.
  3. If it's a fit, we make you a fair offer. There's no obligation, and we never sell your data.
